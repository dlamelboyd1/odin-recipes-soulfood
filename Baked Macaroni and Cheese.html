Baked Macaroni & Cheese

Description:

This rich and creamy variation of macaroni and cheese is truly southern! Perfect for a special holiday side dish, this recipe will please the whole crowd

  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 slices of Bacon *optional
  1. Step 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Step 2. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside *optional
  3. Step 3. In a large pot with boiling salted water cook pasta until al dente. Drain.
  4. Step 4. In a medium skillet saute the chopped onion, and minced garlic. Take off heat and add chopped cooked bacon and set aside.
  5. Step 5. To make the sauce, in a medium saucepan melt the butter or margarine over low heat. Once melted, add the flour and stir constantly for 2 minutes. Gradually add milk and continue stirring until thickened. Stir in 2 cups of the grated Cheddar cheese and stir until melted.
  6. Step 6. Combine cooked pasta, sauteed vegetables and sauce. Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish. Add the last cup of grated Cheddar cheese to top of mixture.
